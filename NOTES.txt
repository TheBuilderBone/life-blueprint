NEXT SESSION — Session 2: Habit Tracking Rebuild
═══════════════════════════════════════
Goal: Replace current habit list with 8 simplified areas
that track real measurable values with visual progress.

Eight habit areas:
💧 Hydration
  - Target: 100 oz/day (adjustable)
  - Log oz per drink throughout day
  - Animated fill rising toward target
  - Shows oz remaining

🥗 Nutrition
  - Target: 2300 cal, 185g P, 230g C, 70g F (adjustable)
  - Manual input: name, calories, macros
  - AI assisted: type food name and portion, AI estimates macros
  - User accepts or adjusts AI estimate before logging
  - Calorie ring + macro bars

💪 Movement
  - Input: activity type, duration, intensity
  - AI estimates calories burned based on activity and body weight
  - Current weight: 215 lbs (adjustable in settings)
  - Shows cal burned, active minutes, activity log

😴 Sleep
  - Input: bedtime, wake time, quality rating, alcohol night before
  - Shows hours slept, quality trend, alcohol correlation over time

💰 Financial Discipline
  - Input: spending decisions, amount saved, impulse purchases
  - Shows daily spend vs budget, running weekly total

🎯 Current Mission
  - User defines project name and current milestone
  - Input: tasks worked on today, time spent, what is next
  - Shows overall completion and last task completed
  - Generic — works for house, business, certification, anything

🧠 Mental Clarity
  - Input: focus level, stress level, what is occupying mind
  - Shows 7 day trend

⏰ Time Management
  - Input: did you execute your plan, what got skipped and why
  - Shows follow-through rate over time

Personal Settings additions:
- Water target (default 100 oz)
- Calorie target (default 2300)
- Macro targets P/C/F
- Body weight (default 215 lbs)
- Current Mission name and milestone
- All values editable at any time

Design direction:
- Each habit card feels alive not flat
- Water: animated fill rising as oz logged
- Calories: ring filling toward target
- Movement: progress bar showing cal burned
- Clean one glance tells you where you stand
